# GH Hostels — Ad Creative Pack v1

> Audiences: hostel owners, managers, university accommodation officers in Ghana
> Channels: Meta (Facebook + Instagram), Google Search, Google Display, TikTok, YouTube Shorts
> Currency: GHS · Geo: Ghana (then Nigeria, Côte d'Ivoire)
> Pacing recommendation: Start at GH₵ 200/day total across 3 ad sets, optimise after 7 days

## 1 · Audience definitions

### A1 — Owner / GM (primary)
- 28-58, Ghana, English
- Job titles: hostel owner, hostel manager, GM, hostel administrator
- Interests: property management, small business, real estate, Paystack, Quickbooks, Sage, Mobile Money
- Behaviours: small business page admins, property investors
- Exclude: students, anyone who already signed up for GH Hostels

### A2 — University accommodation officer (institutional)
- 30-60, lives in Ghana
- Education: university degree
- Workplace targeting: University of Ghana, KNUST, UCC, UEW, GIMPA, Ashesi, UDS, UMaT
- Interests: higher education, student services, university administration

### A3 — Bursar / accountant (financial decision-maker)
- 28-55
- Job titles: accountant, bursar, finance manager, bookkeeper
- Interests: GRA, SSNIT, accounting software, Sage, QuickBooks, payroll
- Useful for retargeting from comparison content

### A4 — Lookalike (cold scale)
- 1-3% lookalike of paying customers (build after 50 paid customers)

### A5 — Retargeting
- Visited site in last 30 days, did not start trial
- Started trial, did not subscribe (day 21 trigger)
- Watched 50%+ of YouTube ad

## 7 · Compliance & policy notes

- Meta: no claims of "guaranteed revenue" or specific outcome promises (e.g., "earn GH₵ X/month")
- Google: no testimonial without verifiable customer consent
- Use ™ or ® only where actually registered (we're not — drop them)
- Currency on creatives must include "GH₵" prefix to avoid USD confusion in ad review
- Mobile Money logos (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo) — get permission before featuring logos prominently; safe to say "MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money supported" in text
- 🇬🇭 flag emoji is fine; "Made in Ghana" claim is verifiable (HQ in Accra)
